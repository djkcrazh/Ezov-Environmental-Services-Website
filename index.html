How It Works

A Clear Process for Every Plastic We Handle

From collection point to recycled material — every step is managed with care and transparency.

01

Request & Pickup

Waste pickups are scheduled and collected from homes, schools, businesses, and job sites on a reliable, agreed timetable.

02

Transportation

Collected plastics are weighed, safely packaged, and transported to our recycling facility at Pokuase-Mayera for processing.

03

Sorting & Processing

Materials are sorted by type and colour, cleaned, and prepared to prevent contamination before moving to the recycling stage.

04

Recycling

Sorted plastics go through a carefully monitored recycling process, producing quality raw materials ready for use by manufacturers.

Our Mission

Responsible Plastic Recovery for a Cleaner Ghana

  • Deliver efficient, eco-friendly plastic waste collection and recycling services.
  • Promote sustainability through waste reduction, reuse, and recycling initiatives.
  • Create employment opportunities for women and youth in waste collection.
  • Educate communities on responsible waste management and environmental stewardship.
  • Ensure compliance with environmental regulations and best industry practices.
